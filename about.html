Mission Statement

Istra – THE REVOLUTION IN TANZANIAN FILMMAKING

Films can be used to change society for the better. There are a lot of issues that happen daily which are not addressed. With the power of the visual medium, iStra aims to provoke meaningful conversations on societal issues.

iStra is a Tanzanian-based platform that provides content and resources to spark consultation, conversation, and constructive dialogue on social issues. The site has an array of tools that steers and gives structure to the discussion.

iStra will have content that will be in formats of live-action and animation. Apart from that, there will also be other content that will be used to foster meaningful conversations.

iStra will be at the forefront of Tanzanian filmmaking as it will allow audiences to connect with stakeholders in tackling issues that impede development. Users will be able to ask questions through a specialized toolkit.

Local content creators will have the opportunity to upload their work, therefore creating an atmosphere where established and upcoming filmmakers can use their unique voices to serve the community.

As a platform made in Tanzania, iStra strives to create an opportunity where people come together and collectively come up with solutions that serve every community member.

iStra endeavors to inspire hope among all circles of the community that life-changing content can be made by Tanzanians for Tanzanians.
